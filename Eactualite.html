ScrumLand's news

Inauguration of the Municipal Park “VerdePrintemps”

Scrumland celebrated the inauguration of the brand new Municipal Park “VerdePrintemps” in the heart of the city. Residents are invited to enjoy the green spaces, modern playgrounds and walking trails. The park promises to be a relaxing place for families and friends.

Economic Development: New Businesses Settling in Scrumland

Scrumland's economic growth continues with new businesses arriving in the area. These investments create job opportunities for residents and strengthen the economic vitality of the city.

Launch of an Innovative Recycling Program

Concerned about the environment, the Municipality of Scrumland has introduced an innovative recycling program aimed at reducing waste production. Residents are encouraged to actively participate in this ecological initiative to preserve the natural beauty of the area.

Success of the “Arts en Scrum” Cultural Festival

The annual “Arts en Scrum” cultural festival was a huge success this year, attracting local and international artists. The city's streets have been transformed into open-air art galleries, offering a rich and diverse cultural experience to all visitors.

Improvements in Health Services

Scrumland Municipality has invested in significant improvements to local health services. A new modern medical center has opened, providing quality care to residents. Wellness and prevention programs are also underway to promote community health.

Downtown Renovation Project

The municipality has unveiled an ambitious renovation project for the city center, aiming to modernize infrastructure while preserving the historic charm of the region. Citizens are invited to share their ideas and suggestions to contribute to the final design of the project.
