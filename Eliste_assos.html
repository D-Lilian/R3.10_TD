List of Associations in ScrumLand:

ScrumArts Cultural Association

Promote visual arts, music and literature through events and workshops.

ScrumLand Student Association (AESL)

Support local students by organizing events, conferences and activities.

Association for the Environment and Sustainable Development (AEDD)

Raise awareness and act to preserve the local environment.

Association of ScrumLand Volunteers (ABS)

Coordinate volunteer initiatives and charitable actions in the city.

ScrumLand Parents Association (APS)

Support parents and organize family activities to strengthen community ties.

Association for Technological Innovation (AIT)

Promote technological education through workshops and events related to technology and innovation.

Association of Happy Senior

Providing support and social activities for the city's elderly.

Encourage creativity and artistic talents among young people by organizing courses and exhibitions.

Association for Equality and Diversity (AED)

Work to promote equal opportunities and diversity within the ScrumLand community.
